About Me

I am a Data Scientist and bioinformatics researcher with broad expertise in genomics, drug discovery, and AI-driven analytics. My work spans mRNA vaccines, genetic and drug perturbation studies, and the development of advanced machine learning and statistical models to extract actionable insights from complex biological and biomedical data.
